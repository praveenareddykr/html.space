Project Description:

McDonalds Application is a Mobile Application for Ordering Food Online in McDonalds store in USA. This Application forge the connections between the customers and McDonalds by providing many facilities. The Customers can place orders with different Pick-Up options like Curbside, Front Counter, Table Services and Drive-Through. This Application also provides facility to the customer to order food before reaching the store thus they get Freshly prepared food by the time they reach Store. This Project also includes Creating and Publishing different types of Offers and Providing Reward Points to the customer which can be used for Free Food. And improving Communication with customers by sending Communications through Emails and Push Notifications.
